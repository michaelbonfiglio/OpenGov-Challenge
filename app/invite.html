Michael Bonfiglio OpenGov Challenge

Email Invite

The example email provided was fairly restrained and I thought this invite email would benefit from a bit of splash to be welcoming, so I used the brighter blues in the color palette and pushed the type hierarchy to have a lot of variation. It needed to have an obvious call to action, which is the big blue Get Started button — I liked "Get Started" it felt friendly and encouraging but I did some research on it and I entertained all of the following:

  • Join the team
  • Accept Invitation
  • Check it out
  • Become an Opengov user

I pulled from the site and other emails I saw for appropriate language and copy length. Since the user persona for the department head mentions that they might not be aware that their municipality is using OpenGov, I thought it would be helpful to include a quick overview at the bottom of the email about what OpenGov is. The imagery is taken from the site.
